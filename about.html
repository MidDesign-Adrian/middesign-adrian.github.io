Philosophy

Living in the
middle of design.

MidDesign is an independent design studio built on the idea of living in the middle of design. Embracing the process, not just the outcome, allows for a deeper exploration of the subject matter. Staying invested in the work and remaining open to the natural flow of ideas creates a more authentic message that ultimately results in a better connection with your audience and a better growing business.

The result is design that doesn't just look good… it works.

Commit

Approach every project with intention, paying attention to each design decision and how it impacts the overall system.

Be Curious

Explore new ideas, technologies, and perspectives to uncover distinctive solutions that push boundaries and elevate user experience.

Have Fun

Design with enthusiasm and authenticity. Designing personally inspired work will ultimately better resonate with audiences and leave lasting impressions.
