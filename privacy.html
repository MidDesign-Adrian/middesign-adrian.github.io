Privacy Policy

How we handle data on this site.

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

MidDesign Studio operates this website at middesignstudio.com. This page explains what data is collected when you visit and how it is used. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with pages. GA4 collects anonymised data including pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location (country/city level). This data is used solely to improve the site experience.

Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to distinguish visitors and sessions. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google Analytics for the purposes described above. No other cookies are used. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Contact Forms

If you submit a message through the contact form, the information you provide (name, email, message) is used only to respond to your enquiry. This data is not stored beyond what is necessary to reply.

Third-Party Links

This site contains links to external websites (LinkedIn, Behance, etc.). MidDesign is not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to adrian.cole@middesignstudio.com.
